Automatic Signal Timing

Congestion during the peak hours mainly at the intersection is the major challenge of the hour.In order to alleviate this problem, automatic traffic diversion plan is introduced to prevent further congestion or accumulation of traffic and speeds up clearance, saving time and fuel and minimizing exhaust emissions.This can be achieved using a microcontroller in the traffic signal of a jammed road which communicates this to the previous traffic signal itself, so that users can automatically take a diversion.
2

Radiation Reflecting Pavements

The goal of this venture is a literally and figuratively cooler pavement.This is done by spreading a thin gray coating of cool seal into the heart of the hottest neighborhood using the principle of reducing the heat absorption in the built environment which means basically boosting the solar reflectivity.
3

Construction Automation

The goal of this venture is faster, simpler and more versatile construction by applying the principles of automated technology in building construction.The aim is to make construction process affordable for mass construction,save time and build versatility into the construction process.This introduces a new technological dimension in civil engineering.
4

Embankments using flexible reinforcement

Have you ever wondered that plastic can be used as a reinforcement !!! Yes! It can be used as a reinforcement if we can identify the direction of its strength.Using the fact that “every material has strength in a specific direction”, here we use the strength of the flexible materials and use them as reinforcement in the structures.
