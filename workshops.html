Bentley Workshop FAQs

Staad Pro Workshop by Bentley

1. Who can participate in these workshops?

This workshop is recommended to be taken up by Civil Engineering students who are currently in 2nd year or above and interested students currently enrolled for MTech in Civil Engineering.

2. What are the requirements for the workshops?

Students are requested to bring their laptops(64-bit processor mandatory) along for the workshop. The training software and other necessary learning aids will be distributed at the venue.

3. What will be the duration of the workshops?

The workshop will be conducted in the morning (check the fest schedule) on the said days **

4. Are these workshops free for participants?

No, they are paid (Rs. 500 for each). *

5. Will accommodation be provided for the stay?

Yes. Please check out our Hospitality Tab for details of the same.

6. Do these workshops have any selection criteria?

No. The registrations will be done by following “first come first served” till the places are filled up.

7. Any specific (content related) prerequisites required and skills gained at the end?

Prior knowledge related to Shear force, Bending Moments, will kick start you into the workshop. We introduce you to Staad Pro software, a powerful analysis and design tool for use in Structural Engineering Important concepts and application of this tool will be explained and demonstrated with a hands-on session.

8. Will certificates be given to everyone?

 Yes. Online Certificates will be given to all participants later (to be downloaded from site itself). 

*A few free workshops are also conducted during the fest. Participants can register for them on-spot during Fest days. (Limited seats only)
